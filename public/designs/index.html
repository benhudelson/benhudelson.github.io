CoachME

AI-powered running coach with adaptive training that listens to your body. A design system built for task-oriented progressive disclosure.

Design Principles

The CoachME app embodies an energetic performance-focused aesthetic with task-oriented progressive disclosure. Bold, vibrant colors drive action and celebrate achievement while maintaining professional usability.

Energetic Performance

Vibrant colors and bold visual hierarchy create an energizing experience that motivates action and celebrates every achievement.

Action-Oriented

Primary red-orange drives immediate action on today's workout and CTAs. Success green rewards completion and positive progress.

Clear Hierarchy

High-contrast colors ensure WCAG AA compliance while guiding attention to what matters most: your next workout.

Performance Mindset

Design elements inspire athletic confidence—energizing without overwhelming, professional without being corporate.

Typography

Pairing DM Serif Display for headlines with Outfit for body text creates a distinctive voice that's both elegant and athletic.

The quick brown fox jumps

DM Serif Display • Display Font • Headlines, Titles, Numbers

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. This is the body text used throughout the application for paragraphs, labels, and UI elements. Outfit provides excellent readability at all sizes.

Screen Designs

Explore the complete MVP screen flows. Each screen is designed with purposeful animations and follows the task-oriented disclosure principle.

📱

Today Screen - Scenario 1

Workout not yet completed. Shows today's hilly steady state run (8mi) with "View Details" button. Includes calendar drawer integration.

✅

Today Screen - Scenario 2

Workout completed but not checked in. Shows "Completed" badge and prominent "How was it?" button to start check-in flow.

📊

Today Screen - Scenario 3

Workout completed and checked in. Shows check-in summary with effort, physical, and mental scores. "Edit Check-in" option available.

🏃

Workout Detail

Full breakdown of workout structure with warmup, main effort, cooldown phases, effort zones, and pacing guidance.

✍️

Post-Workout Check-in

Linear flow with sliders for effort, physical state, and mental state. Completes in under 60 seconds.

🩹

Pain Follow-up

Triggered when pain keywords detected. Captures location, type, severity, and when pain occurs for injury prevention.

💡

Intervention Modal

Rules-based coaching intervention that slides up when triggers detected. Suggests plan adjustments with clear rationale.

📅

Calendar Drawer

Gesture-based swipe-up drawer showing training calendar with horizontal scroll, status indicators, and workout details.

🎯

Onboarding Flow

Full-screen questions capturing goal race, date, experience level, weekly mileage, injury history, and training schedule.
