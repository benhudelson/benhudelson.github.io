Today's Focus

This is a strong effort on hills at 85% intensity. Work into the effort quickly and smoothly, then settle into steady state running. This should feel challenging but controlled—not like racing.

Workout Philosophy

Purpose
Hill Training Benefits

The hill component of this workout puts you into short-term aerobic debt and trains your body to quickly clear lactic acid and return to a sustainable high-end aerobic effort. The muscular tension of hill running trains deeper into the "barrel" of muscle fibers to be ready on race day. Downhill running heightens neuromuscular system activation, enabling you to feel more coordinated running at faster speeds.
